PrivLabs

Offline supply-chain pre-audit assessment platform.

PrivLabs helps security teams, consultants, and decision-makers identify, structure, and communicate risk before scanning, exploitation, or full penetration testing.

Why PrivLabs?

Most incidents originate from weak configurations and trust assumptions: privilege boundaries, update mechanisms, CI/CD pipelines.

PrivLabs focuses on early risk signals, not exploitation — so teams can decide where to focus first.

What it does

  • Linux privilege escalation pre-audit
  • CI/CD pipeline trust analysis
  • Supply-chain configuration reviews
  • Executive-friendly risk summaries

What it is not

No exploitation. No active attacks. No scanning. PrivLabs complements pentesting — it does not replace it.

Trust & Security

Offline analysis only. No data stored, logged, or transmitted. All processing is done in-memory.
